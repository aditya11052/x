History Of X Technologies


X Technologies was founded on November 5th, 2011 by Mike Robite, Esdi Kard and Aditya Mota
in Princeton, New Jersey. X Tech. released the XFONE on December 4th, 2011 with XAppStore and XMusic
followed by the XTAB on December 22nd and the XSET on December 29th. The XFONE was an instant
success with over 25 Million Units Sold From December 4th-December 31st ranking X Tech the
no.1 Smartphone manufacturer in of 2011 Q4 above Apple. Following the success of the XFONE
and the XTAB, X Tech. released the XFONE 2 and XTAB 2 both on June 10th, 2012 and the
XFONE 3 and XTAB 3 on June 12th, 2013. On Janruary 20th, 2014, X Tech made their company
Public and on April 23rd, 2014 the XSET 2 was released. X Tech hit a short roadbump with
the release of the XFONE 4 on June 19th, 2014 and XTAB 4 on August 1st, 2014 as the sales
were at an all-time low. However, on Janurary 15th, 2015 the company regained its success
with the new and improved XFONE IV+ and XTAB IV+. The XFONE V was released on May 26th, 2015
and XTAB V was released on October 7th, 2015. The XFONE 6 was released on August 6th, 2016
and the XTAB 6 was released on October 31st, 2016. In 2017, X Tech focused on Apps
The company released the XCLOUD on Janruary 18th, 2017, XMAPS on August 7th, 2017
and XLIBRARY on October 18th, 2017. The company's newest releases feature the
XFONE 7 on March 12th, 2018, the XTAB 7 on March 26th, 2018 and the XSET 3 on
May 18th, 2018. The CEO Triumvirate announced a press confrence regarding
X Tech's 2019 plans on August 24th, 2018.

ABOUT OUR CEO's

X Technologies was founded by Mike Robite, Esdi Kard and Aditya Mota.
The Three Boys met at Princeton University. The Three were roommates
for all 4 years at the school. When the 3 Graduated in 2010 they
moved into an apartment near the University where they developed the
first prototype for the XFONE. After a successful pitch on Shark Tank
the three boys recieved a deal from Mark Cuban and Mr. Wonderful himself
Kevin O'Leary. Cuban and O'Leary manufactured the devices while the three
focused on developing the devices. After the successful release of the XFONE
Cuban and O'Leary threw a huge party for them in Ceasers Palace, Las Vegas
and the rest is history.

MIKE ROBITE

Mike Robite Was born in Trenton, New Jersey in 1988. He was always number one
on his schools robotics team. He won his first hackaton at the age of 11.
He was named Valedictorian at His Graduation. Mike chose Princeton over
Harvard, MIT and Georgetown. Mike was known around his schools as
"Steve Jobs Jr" and his teachers would often pull him out of his other class
to help them with technological related issues.

ESDI KARD

Esdi Kard Was born in Harrisburg, Pennsylvaia in 1989. As a kid he always
wanted to be a pilot. That was until he met Bill Gates at the age of 7.
That inspired him to enter and pursue technology as his field. Esdi
managed to hack into his Windows 98 Computer and finished as one of the
brightest students in his class. He even skipped 7th grade. In 2010 he
successfully programed a game for IOS which he named Xtraordinary,
which later became the basis for the name oh X Technologies and its slogan.

ADITYA MOTA

Aditya Mota was born in Princeton, New Jersey in 1989. As a child, he
was always considered a prodigy among his peers, teachers and he was
even on the radar of a few Princeton Professers. He always won his schools
Geography Bee and always shocked his teachers with his creative skills.
He is known as the creative mind behind X Technologies. After being named
valedictorian he grew to fame as a recording of his graduation speech
flew across a new popular site named Youtube.
